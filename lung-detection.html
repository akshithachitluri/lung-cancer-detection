About This Tool

This system detects four types of lung conditions:

  • Adenocarcinoma: Most common lung cancer type
  • Large Cell Carcinoma: Aggressive non-small cell cancer
  • Squamous Cell Carcinoma: Often linked to smoking
  • Normal: Healthy lung tissue

Your privacy is important. Images are processed securely and not stored permanently.
